# Product Guide

## Vision
The Plannerâ€™s Assistant is a procedurally explainable planning judgement system designed to build trust between AI and human planners. It primarily supports Local Plan Production and Development Management by providing transparent, legally defensible reasoning traces for complex decisions, rather than "black box" outputs. The system serves as an extensible AI assistant capable of evolving across all tasks required by the planning role.

## Target Users
- **Case Officers (Digital Case Officer UI):** Utilizing the system for development management and decision support.
- **Policy Makers & Local Planning Authorities:** Leveraging the tool for consistent, policy-aligned Local Plan production.

## Core Value Proposition
- **Trust & Transparency:** Delivers a clear, 8-move grammar reasoning trace to ensure every automated or assisted judgement is explainable and legally defensible.
- **Efficiency:** Automates routine planning validations to reduce manual workload.
- **Consistency:** Ensures decision-making remains aligned with local and national policies across the authority.

## Key Features
- **CULP Artefact Gating:** A formal mechanism to track and gate the progression of planning evidence through structured quality checks.
- **Visuospatial Workbench:** Interactive tools for map overlays and visual evidence analysis to ground judgements in spatial reality.
- **ODP Ecosystem Interop:** Seamless integration with existing Digital Planning ecosystem standards (PlanX, BOPS, DPR schemas) to ensure data portability.
- **Extensible Architecture:** Designed to scale from specific tasks to a comprehensive assistant across the full spectrum of planning duties.
